--- tags: - poe - powersupply order: 3 procedures: - title: Reassemble your Home Assistant Yellow steps: - title: Place the translucent enclosure ground plate on the table. image: step-13.png content: |
  • Make sure the two round notches form a line pointing towards the top right corner.
- title: Place the carrier board onto the enclosure ground plate. image: step-14.png content: |
  • Make sure the USB connectors are facing upwards, away from you.
- title: Install the carrier board. image: step-15.png content: |
  • Turn the enclosure upside down.
  • Then, slide the push buttons into the openings of the housing.
- title: Insert the 4 thumb screws. image: step-16.png content: |
  • Do not over-tighten the screws.
- title: Insert Ethernet Cable image: insert-ethernet-cable.png content: |
  • Push the ethernet cable into the jack until you hear a click.
  • The other end of the Ethernet cable should be connected to your Router or Switch with POE.
