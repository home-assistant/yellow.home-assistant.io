--- layout: guide description: Home Assistant Yellow guideName: Installing Home Assistant software on Yellow Kit procedures: - title: Setting up the hardware steps: - title: Gather the required material. preTitle: Step 1 image: unbox-yellow-std.png content: |
  • Fully assembled Home Assistant Yellow Kit
  • Ethernet cable
  • Power supply (not required for PoE variant)
  • USB thumb drive
  • Computer with Internet access
- title: On your computer, install the Raspeberry Pi Imager preTitle: Step 2 image: rpi_imager.png content: |
  • as described under https://www.raspberrypi.com/software/
- title: Insert the USB drive into your computer, preTitle: Step 3 - title: Start the Raspberry Pi Imager. preTitle: Step 4 image: start_rpi_imager.png - title: In Raspberry Pi Imager, choose the button Choose OS. preTitle: Step 5 image: rpi_choose_ha_os.png content: |
  • From the dropdown menu, select Home Assistant Operating System.
- title: Choose the button Choose Storage. preTitle: Step 6 image: rpi_choose_storage.png content: |
  • From the dropdown menu, select your USB drive.
- title: Choose the Write button. preTitle: Step 7 image: rpi_choose_write.png content: |
  • Wait for the Home Assistant Operating system to be written to the USB drive.
- title: Eject the USB drive from your computer. preTitle: Step 8 - title: Insert the USB drive with the Home Assistant Operating System image into Home Assistant Yellow Kit. preTitle: Step 9 image: insert_usb.png - title: Connect the Ethernet cable to the Ethernet connector on Home Assistant Yellow. preTitle: Step 10 image: insert_ethernet_cable.png - title: If you do not have PoE, preTitle: Step 11 image: insert_power_cable.png content: |
  • Connect the power supply to Home Assistant Yellow Kit.
- title: Wait for Home Assistant Yellow to start booting from the USB drive. preTitle: Step 12 image: status_leds_yellow_heartbeat.gif content: |
  • During boot, the Yellow LED blinks in a heartbeat pattern.
  • Once the installer is ready, the Yellow LED is on constantly.
- title: Press the red or the blue button. preTitle: Step 13 image: status_leds_yellow_blinking_all_off.gif content: |
  • Installation of Home Assistant OS starts automatically.
  • The yellow LED starts to blink steadily.
  • Once all the LEDs turn off, the flashing process is finished.
- title: Remove the USB flash drive and disconnect power. preTitle: Step 14 image: insert-ethernet-cable.png content: |
  • If you do not have PoE:
  • Remove the power cable.
- title: If you do have PoE, preTitle: Step 15 image: no_cables_no_usb.png content: |
  • Also remove the Ethernet cable.
- title: Reconnect power. preTitle: Step 16 image: insert-power.png content: |
  • If you do not have PoE:
  • Reinsert the power cable to start Home Assistant.
- title: If you do have PoE, preTitle: Step 17 image: insert-ethernet-cable.png content: |
  • Reinsert the Ethernet cable.
- title: Get started with Home Assistant. preTitle: Step 17 image: ha_onboarding.png content: |
  • Initial startup may take longer - depending on your internet connection.
  • Visit http://homeassistant.local:8123 in your Browser to access the device Web frontend.
  • The Web frontend guides you through the initial onboarding.
  • For further information, refer to the instructions provided under https://www.home-assistant.io/getting-started/onboarding/.
